SoCal AI Responsibility Summit (SAIRS)

The SoCal AI Responsibility Summit (SAIRS) was one of the largest student-organized AI events in California, gathering over 400 participants from 17 different academic institutions, 10 speakers, 10 mentors, and 8 sponsors.
