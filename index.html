How it Works

Bridging the gap between industry and students.

1. Industry

We connect with businesses and organizations who are looking for software solutions. We aim to be as inclusive and accommodating as possible, no organization is too large or too small to work with us.

2. Students

We match projects with our network of high achieving student experts. Our students are carefully selected by our recruiting team and represent the most experienced and talented software engineers at UCSB.

3. Solutions

Students build the solutions organizations are looking for, meticulously crafted to their exact specifications. We present our work with our organizations frequently and iterate with feedback until our clients are satisfied.
