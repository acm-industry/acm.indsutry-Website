Unlock a wealth of knowledge with our frequently asked questions, designed to empower students in mastering the art of AI

HOW DO I BECOME A MEMBER

To become a member of Bruin AI, apply online, submit your resume, and attend the interview process to showcase your passion and commitment.
‍
Join our vibrant community of AI enthusiasts today!

ARE THERE ANY PREREQUISITES OR SKILLS REQUIRED TO JOIN

There are no specific prerequisites or skills required to join Bruin AI. We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels.

We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive community of AI enthusiasts.

WHEN IS YOUR RECRUITMENT CYCLE

Our recruitment cycle typically begins at the start of each quarter. Applications for membership are open for the first two weeks, and students will be notified of their acceptance by the start of week 3.

Make sure to mark your calendar and don't miss the opportunity to join Bruin AI during our recruitment period!

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS TO REMAIN AN ACTIVE MEMBER

To remain an active member of Bruin AI, students are required to attend a minimum of three workshops per quarter to ensure continuous learning and engagement.

Additionally, active participation in team projects is expected, as members are encouraged to contribute their skills and expertise to the collaborative AI initiatives.

DOES THE CLUB HAVE PARTNERSHIPS WITH OTHER ORGANIZATIONS

Yes, Bruin AI actively cultivates partnerships with various organizations. We have established collaborations with numerous clubs, fostering a vibrant network of connections within the AI community.

As a result, we frequently organize cross-over professional and social events with our partners, providing valuable opportunities for networking and expanding your AI horizons.

WHAT MAKES BRUIN AI SPECIAL

What makes Bruin AI truly special is our exclusive focus on applying AI to improve people's lives. As the only club on campus dedicated to this mission, we strive to make a positive impact by leveraging AI across various domains.

From healthcare to education, our intention is to explore the infinite possibilities of AI application. Join us to be part of a transformative journey where AI meets real-world challenges, creating a better future for all.
