Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

keyboard_arrow_down

Submit your resume to the "Apply Now" form above and we will reach out to schedule an interview. If it is not there, we are not currently looking for additional applicants. Check back later and we would be excited to have you join us!

Are there any prerequisites or skills required to join?

keyboard_arrow_down

There are no specific prerequisites or skills required to join GauchoAI. We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels! All we require is that you are a passionate problem solver!

Does the club have partnerships with other organizations?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! We are a branch of ACM at UCSB, and are a sister-club of BruinAI at UCLA!

What makes GauchoAI special?

keyboard_arrow_down

GauchoAI is the only tech-focused industry project club at UCSB. We know how hard it can be to bridge the gap between academics and industry. Our unique industry project series is a great way to expand your portfolio, industry expertise, and connections!
