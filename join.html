Join our vibrant community of AI enthusiasts today!

ARE THERE ANY PREREQUISITES OR SKILLS REQUIRED TO JOIN?

There are no specific prerequisites or skills required to join Bruin AI. We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels.

We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive community of AI enthusiasts.

WHEN IS YOUR RECRUITMENT CYCLE?

Our recruitment cycle typically occurs once a year during the fall to ensure proper training, project assignments, and bonding with recruitment classes.

Make sure to mark your calendar and don't miss the opportunity to join Bruin AI during our recruitment period!

DOES THE CLUB HAVE PARTNERSHIPS WITH OTHER ORGANIZATIONS?

Bruin AI has partners of all sizes from startups like GumGum to corporations like Cisco.  We’ve additionally partnered with Organizations like AI LA and UCLA, startups Humane, Kindo, and UpSkill.
‍
Whether it’s teaching students about the latest AI developments or industry projects, we aim to create opportunities in AI for everyone in our vast talent network.

WHAT MAKES BRUIN AI SPECIAL?

What makes Bruin AI truly special is our exclusive focus on applying AI to improve people's lives. As the only club on campus dedicated to this mission, we strive to make a positive impact by leveraging AI.

From healthcare to education, our intention is to explore the infinite possibilities of AI application. Join us to be part of a transformative journey where AI meets real-world challenges
